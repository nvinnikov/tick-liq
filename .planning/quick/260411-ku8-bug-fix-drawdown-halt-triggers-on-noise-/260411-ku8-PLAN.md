before the first WS price update. Any small price movement then calculates a massive drawdown
percentage (e.g., 76%), triggering an immediate halt. This makes shadow mode unusable with
--max-drawdown enabled.

Output: Patched risk_monitor.rs with MIN_PEAK_USD = 1.0 threshold; updated test coverage.
</objective>
